About Me

I am a principal researcher at the Networking Research Group at Microsoft Research Redmond (MSR). Prior to joining MSR Redmond, I have worked at Microsoft Research India, and Bosch Research and Technology Centera in Palo Alto, CA, USA. I graduated from University of Southern Californiawith a PhD in Computer Science in 2006. My advisor was Prof. Ramesh Govindan. Prior to my PhD I obtained my Masters in Electrical Engineering (Signal Processing and Control Systems) from Drexel University in 2000 and B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from IIT Varanasi.

Background and Research Interests

By training and experience, I have a background in Networking, AI, Signal Processing and Wireless Communication. My research interests include, Networking and IoT, AI and Video Analytics, Gaming AR/VR,.
