I believe that the greatest gift is to be able to learn music. I am glad my father set me on this path even though my parents never knew much about music.
I am trained in Hindustani Classical Music. Music is a shared family passion and it helps me relax when I need to.

Physics

Physics is my first love. I believe beauty in Physics comes from how succintly it explains the universe. Even though I never ended up pursuing Physics as my career, I love studying Physics even after all these years -- after all you can never forget your first love! Please visit my old Physics Problem's Website if you are a Physics enthusiast or taking part in the Physics Olympiad!

Solving & Creating Puzzles

The rush you feel when you finally solve a confounding puzzle cannot be put into words, it can only be experienced. I love creating puzzles for kids. It's amazing to see the same excitement in their eyes when they solve it. Here are some examples of puzzles I created for kids. Adults may enjoy them too!
Harry Potter's Cryptic Message, Siddhacharya's Amazing Puzzle, Not British, Raga Man, Sort the Elusive, Wild Life - I, Wild Life - II, Sounds Like?, Geometric Figures, Aminography

Travelling

Such a large world and so little time to see it! I love travelling and experiencing new places and cultures. There is so much to learn at every new place -- sights, sounds, philosophies, and beliefs. It lets me take a step back and look at Earth from a distance and suddenly everyday problems seem to melt away.

Photography

A photograph captures a single moment and stops time. It lets one experience that single moment in great detail and depth. I enjoy capturing portraits and details in nature with my SLR camera and reliving the emotions and beauty around me.
